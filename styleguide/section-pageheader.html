15 #pageheader Page header

15.1 #pageheader.1 Basic

The page container should have a center block within. See center section for more info.

The .page-header has white background and a border bottom.

.page-header__section--margin

Example

Page header

<header class="page-header">
  <div class="center center-max">
    <div class="page-header__container">
      <div class="page-header__title">
        <h1 class="heading-size-3 text-ellipsis">Page header</h1>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</header>
