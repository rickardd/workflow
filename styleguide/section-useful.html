22 #useful Useful classes

Commonly used classes that can be used across the app.

22.1 #useful.general Genaral

.float-left- d .center - d .float-right - d .break - Words that doesn't fit on the line will brake to new line .nowrap - Words that doesn't fit will cut with three ending dots. .full-width - Width 100% .clearfix - Put on on parent if children has are floating elements. .relative - Position relative .clickable - Cursor pointer .noselect - Prevent user from selecting text

22.3 #useful.visibility Hide and show

  • .hide
    hide item
  • .show
    show item
  • .hide-max
    hides on screens smaller than breakpoint max
  • .show-max
    shows on screens bigger than breakpoint max
  • .hide-med
    hides on screens smaller than breakpoint med
  • .show-med
    shows on screens bigger than breakpoint med
  • .hide-min
    hides on screens smaller than breakpoint min
  • .show-min
    shows on screens bigger than breakpoint min
  • .hide-mob
    hides on screens smaller than breakpoint mob
  • .show-mob
    shows on screens bigger than breakpoint mob
